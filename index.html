What is PetAid?

PetAid is a mission-driven emergency service that connects pet parents with real-time ambulances, nearby pet hospitals, and stores pet health records in one easy-to-use app. With our platform, your pet gets fast care when they need it most — anytime, anywhere.

Why PetAid?

Emergency Pet Ambulance

Instantly book an ambulance in a pet emergency — fast, trusted, and nearby.

Digital Medical & Vaccination Records

Store and access your pet's full medical history anytime.

Live Hospital Connectivity

Our network links your pet to nearby partner vet hospitals in real-time.

Pet Condition Updates During Travel

Get updates on your pet's health condition while in transit to the hospital.
